Connect to Wi-Fi

To receive updates and ensure the best experience, please connect your headset to WiFi before starting the MIND app. The headset will automatically update the app when connected to WIFI.

  1. 1 To connect to Wi-Fi, press the Meta button located in the right controller. A menu bar will appear. Use the controller as a laser pointer to select the fast settings (time, WIFI, and battery) and press the trigger button.
  2. 2 Use the controller as a laser pointer to select Wi-Fi.
  3. 3 Find your Wi-Fi network and enter the password using the controller and the virtual keyboard.

Open MIND App

  1. 1 Press the Meta button located in the right controller. A menu bar will appear. Use the controller as a laser pointer to select the library icon and press the trigger button.
  2. 2 Look for the MIND application, point at it with the controller, and click on it.
  3. 3 The screen will turn black, then display a green background with number inputs. Congratulations you are now in the MIND app.

Troubleshooting

Headset won't turn on? - Charge the Headset

Like a smart phone, the headset is charged using USB-C.

  1. 1 Locate the included power brick and USB-C cable.
  2. 2 Connect the USB-C cable to the power brick.
  3. 3 Plug the USB-C cable into the port on the left side of the headset.

Controller not working? - Replace Controller Batteries

  1. 1 Press the triangle button to open the battery cover.
  2. 2 Remove the cover to access the battery.
  3. 3 Replace the battery making sure the positive (+) is pointing down.

Need more help? - Restart is the solution!

Restart Your Headset

- Hold the power button (right side) until the Power Off screen appears, then select Restart.

Force Restart

- Hold the power button for 30 seconds, wait 30 seconds, then press it again to turn on.
